Privacy policy

General

Decent Call Blocker Pro is developed by one main developer, with a few people helping from time to time, mostly with translations.

Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using Decent Call Blocker Pro, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

Decent Call Blocker Pro does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The 'Read Contacts' permission is used for accessing the specific Contacts from your phone, that you want to block number on device.
The 'Read  Phone State' permission is used for checking/trigger the incomming calls so that can be blocked through  this app.
The 'Phone Call' permission will check the phone number of incomming calls to match it with blocked number.
The 'Modify  Phone State' permission is used for changing the state of incomming calls so that can be blocked through this app.
